I work on tropical convection, moisture organization, and the dynamics of high column water vapor (“vapor lakes”) in the Western Equatorial Indian Ocean. My research focuses on understanding how these systems persist and propagate, using reanalysis data and Lagrangian frameworks.

Research

My current work examines the spatial relationship between precipitation and column water vapor, with a focus on persistent high-CWV regions.

  • Lagrangian motion and moisture budget of vapor lakes
  • Role of horizontal vs vertical advection at the lake margin
  • Interaction with East African rainfall
